  <h2 style={{marginTop: 0}}>Overview</h2>

  The **Easium Set** is the entry-level ore set in Another Quality Ore Set. Found exclusively in the **Overworld**, Easium ore generates in the stone layer, making it accessible early in your Minecraft journey.

  Easium provides a solid stepping stone between vanilla diamond gear and the more advanced ore sets. Its tools and armor offer improved durability and efficiency compared to iron, making it an excellent choice for players just beginning to explore the mod's content.
</div>

## Obtaining Easium

Easium ore can be found in the Overworld, typically at Y levels similar to iron ore. Mine the ore with an iron pickaxe or better, then smelt the raw easium in a furnace to obtain Easium Ingots.
