  The **Medium Set** is the second tier ore set in Another Quality Ore Set. Found exclusively in the **Nether**, Medium ore generates throughout the dangerous hellscape dimension, rewarding brave adventurers who dare to explore.

  Medium gear offers significantly improved stats over Easium, making the trek to the Nether worthwhile. The set provides enhanced durability, mining speed, and combat effectiveness.
</div>

## Set Bonus: Fire Resistance

:::tip Set Bonus
When wearing the **full Medium armor set**, you gain permanent **Fire Resistance**! This makes you immune to fire and lava damage, perfect for navigating the treacherous Nether terrain.
:::

## Obtaining Medium

Medium ore can be found scattered throughout the Nether dimension. Mine the ore with an Easium pickaxe or better, then smelt the raw medium in a furnace to obtain Medium Ingots.
