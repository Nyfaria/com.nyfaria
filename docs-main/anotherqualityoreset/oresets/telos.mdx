  <h2 style={{marginTop: 0}}>Overview</h2>

  The **Telos Set** is the ultimate tier in Another Quality Ore Set. Unlike other ore sets, Telos cannot be mined from the world. Instead, it must be **crafted from the other three ore sets**, requiring mastery of all dimensions and materials.

  Telos represents the pinnacle of crafting achievement, combining the power of Easium, Medium, and Hardium into gear that transcends them all.
</div>

## Set Bonuses: Ultimate Power

:::tip Set Bonuses
The **full Telos armor set** provides the most powerful bonuses in the mod:
- **Indestructible**: Telos gear never breaks! It has infinite durability, making it truly eternal.
- **Fire Resistance**: Complete immunity to fire and lava damage
- **Elytra Flight**: The ability to fly as if wearing an Elytra, without needing one!
:::

Telos gear is the ultimate reward for dedicated players who have conquered all three dimensions and mastered the crafting of each ore tier.

## Crafting Telos

To craft Telos gear, you must first create Telos Scrap. This requires rods from all three ore sets and a Dragon Breath, symbolizing the ultimate combination of all the mod's materials.

### Telos Scrap

This is the key ingredient for all Telos gear. It requires rods from Easium, Medium, and Hardium, plus a Dragon Breath as the catalyst.

<MinecraftRecipe
  pattern={["MEH", "EDE", "HEM"]}
