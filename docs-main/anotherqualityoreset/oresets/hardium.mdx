  The **Hardium Set** is the third tier ore set in Another Quality Ore Set. Found exclusively in **The End**, Hardium ore generates on the outer End islands, requiring players to defeat the Ender Dragon and explore beyond the main island.

  Hardium gear represents a significant power increase, offering exceptional durability and effectiveness. This set is designed for end-game players who have conquered The End and seek the ultimate tools and armor.

## Set Bonus: Fire Resistance + Elytra Flight

:::tip Set Bonus
When wearing the **full Hardium armor set**, you gain two powerful bonuses:
- **Fire Resistance**: Complete immunity to fire and lava damage
- **Elytra Flight**: The ability to fly as if wearing an Elytra, without needing one!
:::

This makes the Hardium set incredibly versatile, combining protection, fire immunity, and flight into one powerful armor set.

## Obtaining Hardium

Hardium ore can be found on the outer End islands, accessible via End Gateways after defeating the Ender Dragon. Mine the ore with a Medium pickaxe or better, then smelt the raw hardium in a furnace to obtain Hardium Ingots.
