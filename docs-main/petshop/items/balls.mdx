**Balls** are play items that your pets can interact with! Throw them and watch your pets chase after them.

## Available Balls

- **Tennis Ball** - A classic ball that dogs will fetch and bring back to you
- **Yarn Ball** - A soft ball that dogs will fetch and cats will bat around and play with

## Obtaining

Balls can be purchased from the **Pet Groomer** villager.
