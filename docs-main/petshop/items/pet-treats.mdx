**Pet Treats** are special food items that attract your pets and make them display cute animations when held!

## Available Treats

- **Dog Treat** - A tasty bone-shaped treat for dogs
- **Tuna Treat** - A fishy snack that cats love
- **Peanut** - A crunchy treat for birds
- **Chicken Drumstick** - A meaty treat for dragons
- **Pancakes** - A sweet treat that Derpy Ghosts adore

## Obtaining

Pet Treats can be purchased from the **Pet Groomer** villager.
