The **Black Tuxedo** cat sports an elegant black and white coat pattern that resembles formal evening wear. Their distinctive markings make them look perpetually dressed for a fancy occasion.

Tuxedo cats are known for being exceptionally friendly and intelligent. They're often very vocal and love to communicate with their owners. Their dapper appearance and charming personalities make them wonderful companions.
