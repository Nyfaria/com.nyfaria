The **American Shorthair** is a classic domestic cat breed known for its round face, short ears, and muscular build. These cats come in a wide variety of colors and patterns.

American Shorthairs are easygoing and adaptable cats that get along well with everyone. They're playful but not hyperactive, making them perfect companions for a relaxed Minecraft home.
