The **Calico** cat features a distinctive tri-color coat pattern of white, orange, and black patches. Almost all calico cats are female, making them unique and special.

Calico cats are known for their spirited and independent personalities. They're often described as having "cattitude" but are also affectionate with their owners. Their beautiful patchwork coats make them stand out in any home.
