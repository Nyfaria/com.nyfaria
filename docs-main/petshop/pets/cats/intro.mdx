Cats are independent yet affectionate companions in Pet Shop! They have their own unique behaviors that cat lovers will appreciate.

## Cat Features

- **Follow Mode** - Cats will follow you when set to follow mode
- **Play With Yarn** - Cats will play with a Yarn Ball! Throw a yarn ball and watch them pick it up and bat it around
- **Sleep** - Cats love to sleep in pet beds, especially during the day
- **Hunger & Thirst** - Feed your cats with kibble and provide water in pet bowls
- **Treats** - Give your cats tuna treats to attract their attention
- **Petting** - Right-click to pet your cat and show them some love

## Available Breeds

Pet Shop currently includes the following cat breeds:

- **American Shorthair** - A classic domestic cat with a round face and muscular build
- **Calico** - A distinctive tri-color cat with white, orange, and black patches
- **Black Tuxedo** - An elegant cat with formal black and white markings
- **Brown Tuxedo Munchkin** - An adorable short-legged cat with a stylish brown tuxedo coat
