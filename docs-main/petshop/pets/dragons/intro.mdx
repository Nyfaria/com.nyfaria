Dragons are tiny magical companions in Pet Shop! These adorable little creatures may be small, but they have big personalities.

## Dragon Features

- **Follow Mode** - Dragons will follow you when set to follow mode
- **Flight** - Dragons can fly around, hovering near their owner
- **Hunger & Thirst** - Feed your dragons and provide water in pet bowls
- **Treats** - Give your dragons chicken drumsticks as treats to attract their attention
- **Petting** - Right-click to pet your dragon and show them some love

## Available Dragons

Pet Shop currently includes the following dragons:

- **Aurora** - A beautiful purplish-pink dragon with a magical shimmer
- **Mossflare** - A stunning greenish-blue dragon with an earthy charm
