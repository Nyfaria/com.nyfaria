The **Black and White Husky** features the classic husky coloration with a striking contrast between their dark and light fur. This iconic look makes them one of the most recognizable dog breeds.

These beautiful dogs are known for their friendly demeanor and high energy levels. They love to run, play, and explore alongside their owner, making them perfect adventure companions.
