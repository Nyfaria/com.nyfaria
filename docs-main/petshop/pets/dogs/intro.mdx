Dogs are loyal and energetic companions in Pet Shop! They offer a variety of interactive features that make them feel like real pets.

## Dog Features

- **Follow Mode** - Dogs will follow you wherever you go, staying close by your side
- **Play Fetch** - Throw a tennis ball or yarn ball and watch your dog chase it down and bring it back to you
- **Dig Up Blocks** - Dogs can dig up suspicious blocks for you, helping you find hidden treasures
- **Sleep** - Dogs will sleep in pet beds at night when set to wander mode
- **Hunger & Thirst** - Keep your dog fed with kibble and watered using pet bowls
- **Treats** - Give your dogs dog treats to attract their attention

## Available Breeds

Pet Shop currently includes the following dog breeds:

- **Sable Husky** - A stunning wolf-like dog with a beautiful sable coat
- **Black and White Husky** - The classic husky with striking contrast markings
- **Super Mutt** - A lovable mixed breed with unique characteristics
- **Sheltie** - An intelligent herding dog with a flowing coat
- **English Cocker Spaniel** - A cheerful sporting dog with silky ears
