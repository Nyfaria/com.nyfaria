The **Sheltie** (Shetland Sheepdog) is a beautiful herding dog with a long, flowing coat and an intelligent expression. Originally bred for herding sheep, Shelties are known for their agility and obedience.

These loyal dogs are incredibly smart and eager to please. Their gentle nature and stunning appearance make them a popular choice for pet owners who want a devoted companion.
