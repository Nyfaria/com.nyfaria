Birds are colorful and social companions in Pet Shop! Parrots offer unique features that set them apart from other pets.

## Bird Features

- **Shoulder Perching** - Right-click your parrot while it's on stay mode to place it on your shoulder
- **Bird Cages** - Put parrots in decorative bird cages by right-clicking an empty cage with a parrot on your shoulder
- **Treats** - Give your parrots peanuts as treats to attract their attention
- **Follow Mode** - Parrots will fly alongside you when set to follow mode
- **Stay Mode** - Keep your parrot in one place when you need them to wait

## Available Breeds

Pet Shop currently includes the following parrot varieties:

- **White Striped Parrot** - Beautiful white stripe patterns across colorful plumage
- **Gold Dashed Parrot** - Stunning golden accents that shimmer beautifully
- **Red Accent Albino Parrot** - A rare albino parrot with vibrant red highlights
- **Tropical Parrot** - A dazzling array of tropical colors reminiscent of rainforests
