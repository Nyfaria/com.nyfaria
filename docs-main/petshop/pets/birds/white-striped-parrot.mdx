The **White Striped Parrot** features beautiful white stripe patterns across its colorful plumage. These elegant birds make a stunning addition to any Minecraft home.

Like all parrots in Pet Shop, you can place them on your shoulder or keep them in a bird cage. They're social birds that love attention and will brighten up any room with their presence.
