The **Pet Groomer** villager is responsible for selling many items in the mod, including:

- Kibble
- Playing items (tennis ball, yarn ball, etc.)
- Pet bowls
- Pet beds
- Leashes (all dye colors)
- Pet Doors (all plank and concrete types)

## Using the Grooming Station

You have your pet, which is great; how can you upgrade it even further? How aboutâ€¦ giving your pets cosmetics?

With the new **Grooming Station** block, you can decorate your pet with some cool cosmetics!

### How to Use

1. Put your pet item (making sure it contains your pet) in the top left slot
2. Put your wool (the cost) in the bottom right slot
3. Click one of the options that displays on the left:
   - **Boots**
   - **Collars**
   - **Hats**
4. Take the new pet item that displays on the right
