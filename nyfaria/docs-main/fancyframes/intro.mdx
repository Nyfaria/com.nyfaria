**Fancy Frames** is a mod that adds new painting and item frame variants to Minecraft! These frames are designed to glam up your rooms, bases, and display areas while seamlessly fitting into Vanilla Minecraft.

There is a frame for each Minecraft tier: wood, stone, copper, gold, iron, diamond, emerald, and netherite.
